Our Story

TrueLau began with something small — quite literally: trays of vibrant, nutrient-packed microgreens grown with care, curiosity, and the dream of making healthy eating beautiful, accessible, and exciting.

Right now, we’re at our Proof of Concept stage — testing, refining, and learning every day. But our vision stretches far beyond these first leaves.

We dream of building Phil’s Farm into a diverse, sustainable food ecosystem: expanding from microgreens into innovative farming techniques, growing fresh vegetables and herbs year-round, and creating a laid‑back farm space where people can visit, taste, and connect with their food at its source.

Every sprout we grow today is a seed for the future — a future where healthy living isn’t just a choice, but a lifestyle worth celebrating. Small Leaves. Big Impact. That’s not just our tagline; it’s our journey.
